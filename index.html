About Us

Ruthi’s Pup Spa is a locally owned and operated grooming boutique in Oklahoma City. Our mission is simple: provide a calm, loving environment where your pup can look and feel their best. We use gentle, natural products and take the time to get to know each dog’s unique personality. Whether your furry friend needs a quick wash or a full pampering session, our experienced team will ensure they leave wagging their tail.
